How it works

Simple rules, big bragging rights

A private prediction league across the entire tournament.

Group Stage

All Round 1 picks must be in before the first kick-off. Same rule applies to Rounds 2 & 3 — once the whistle blows, that round is locked.

Knockout Stage

Group-stage predictions come first. Knockout picks open once the fixtures have both teams decided.

Points System

3 pts for the correct result (win/draw/loss).
+2 bonus pts for the exact scoreline. Nothing for a wrong result.
